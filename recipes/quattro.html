Quattro formaggi

Description

Quattro formaggi is a type of pasta traditionally made in Italy, made from combining pasta with four different types of cheeses, usually gorgonzola, fontina, and parmigiano-reggiano

I love pasta so much it's unreal.

Ingredients

  • pasta(spaghetti/spaghettoni)
  • gorgonzola
  • pecorino romano
  • fontina
  • parmigiano-reggiano
